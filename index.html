Genie

The first step in realizing your dreams is to record all your wishes.
Genie does exactly that!

Genie let's you categories your wishes.
Either select from the defualt categories or create your own.

A very simple and easy to use UI with following features:

  1. Add new wish with category, description and deadline.
  2. Pre-created categories to choose from.
  3. Create new Category.
  4. Edit wishes.
  5. Swipe right to delete wishes.

Make a Wish and Genie will fulfill them all!
